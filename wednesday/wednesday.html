screen division

In bootstrap we have 6 dimension

  • (breakpoint name)extra small --> (class infinix) none --> (dimensions) < 576px
  • (breakpoint name)small --> (class infinix)sm --> (dimensions) ≥ 576px
  • medium --> (class infinix) md --> (dimensions) ≥ 768px
  • (breakpoint name)large --> (class infinix)lg --> (dimensions) ≥ 992px
  • (breakpoint name)extra large --> (class infinix)xl --> (dimensions) ≥ 1200px
  • (breakpoint name)extra extra large --> (class infinix)xxl --> (dimensions) ≥ 1400px
